How To Play

  • Press Right Arrow to Move Right.
  • Press Left Arrow to Move Left.
  • Press Up Arrow to Jump.
  • Press Down Arrow to stand still.
  • Press R to throw fireball
  • Press Space to do a rolling attack
  • If the player score is greater than the target score, player wins
  • If the player score is less than the target score, player loses
